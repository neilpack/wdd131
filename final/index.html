About Me

I am a 23-year-old student at BYU-Idaho, based in the Rexburg, Idaho area. Photography has been a passion of mine for over five years, and during that time, I have built a diverse portfolio with hundreds of photos. I have experience capturing portraits, family sessions, and special events, working with a variety of clients to create meaningful and lasting memories. Whether it’s the stunning Idaho landscapes or the special moments in people’s lives, I strive to bring creativity and professionalism to every shoot.
