About Me

I am a 23-year-old student at BYU-Idaho, based in the Rexburg, Idaho area. Photography has been a passion of mine for over five years, and during that time, I have built a diverse portfolio with hundreds of photos. I have experience capturing portraits, family sessions, and special events, working with a variety of clients to create meaningful and lasting memories. If you're interested in capturing special moments with a creative and professional touch, send me a message on Instagram or Facebook to schedule a session!
